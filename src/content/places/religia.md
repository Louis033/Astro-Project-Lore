## Background 
Religia is a monarch kingdom rule by the Holy emperor, Austin Godpower, the level 4 Hero that lead and win the war of the battle of the old world where it was said was a worst war even happened in the history of Aesleth. He stood as the strongest and build a new kingdom around the [Life Tree].

The emperor makes his kingdom to suit the [Life Tree] appereances. So he build the white castle near the tree and it has many mirror so it could reflect the glowing of the tree too. 

Religia has 3 district : Cloud District, Economic District, and Worker Disctrict

## Worker Disctrict
Worker District is a place for a hard laborer and lower income of the Religia. It build to accomodate like factories, a farming house, and many other production sector. The worker district, even if it implies no so honored in Religia Society, is very competitive. If you can't work to meet the employer standard, you can't be fired. 

Almost every bussiness owner in the Worker District want to prove themself to be able to be proved to Economic District. 

## Economic District
Economic District is the busiest place on the entire Aesleth. The Economic machine never sleep, it generate so much income it makes many commoners to dream to make it to the Economics District. 

Religia's Economic Disctrict brand itself to be the best. To be able to own bussiness in Economic District you have to own a license, provided by the Religia Government on the Cloud District. It have to meet a high requirement and standard to be proved, you can't bribe your way there because it's very easy to take down your business. Everyone in Aesleth know your quality is the best if you're settled in Economic District. 

## Cloud District
Cloud District is the holy ground of Religia. It is the place for the monarch and the religion's worker. Even the non member of Cloud District have to meet the rules of behavior or you are to be killed. 

White Castle are on Cloud District, where the noble and most elite soldier in all Aesleth also lived. 

The central government is also in cloud district, the place where you could get the license for everything in Religia.
